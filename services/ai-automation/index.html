Platform Comparison

Why n8n Over Zapier for Most Startups

Zapier works well at low volume. At 1,000 tasks per month you're paying roughly $50/month. At 10,000 tasks — which a single busy CRM workflow can hit easily — you're paying $250–$500/month and climbing. The pricing is designed around task count, so the more useful your automations become, the more expensive they get.

n8n self-hosted flips that model. You run it on a $6/month VPS (we can handle that infrastructure), pay nothing per task, and get full source access to every workflow. Your data never leaves your server — which matters for teams handling customer PII or operating under compliance requirements.

n8n also supports JavaScript and Python execution nodes natively. That means you can write real transformation logic inside the workflow, call internal APIs, or manipulate data structures that would require a paid Zapier add-on or a custom Code step. For engineering-led teams, this is the bigger unlock.

We work with both Zapier and n8n depending on your situation — but for startups running their own infrastructure, n8n is usually the right call. Tinaht manages the hosting and keeps it running, so you get the cost advantage without the ops overhead.
